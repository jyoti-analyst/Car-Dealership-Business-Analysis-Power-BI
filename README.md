# Car-Dealership-Business-Analysis-Power-BI
This project analyzes dealership inventory performance, sales revenue, order delays, and after-sales service using Power BI to identify operational inefficiencies and revenue opportunities.
---

## 📊 Business KPIs Analyzed
- Total Revenue
- Inventory Turnover
- Inventory Health Ratio
- Stockout Frequency
- Dealership Mismatch %
- Delay %
- Service Rate
- After-Sales Revenue
---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- ₹9.7B total revenue generated
- Inventory turnover rate: 7.48
- 81% of orders experienced delays
- Toyota leads in after-sales service revenue
- Model_6 recorded the highest sales volume
- 337K units identified under dead stock risk
---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations
- Reduce high order delay rate through dealership allocation optimization
- Focus marketing on high-turnover models
- Improve inventory mismatch monitoring
- Reduce dead stock risk through demand-based replenishment planning
---
